Hi, I'm Aaron Gregory. I'm a doctoral student in Applied Mathematics working in TEALab at Stony Brook University. My research focus is on theory-motivated improvements in quantum and parallel computing.

My past work includes the design of novel algorithms for resource-constrained scheduling and parallel stencil computations, analysis of continuous- and discrete-time quantum walks, writing CAS codes to solve systems of nonlinear PDEs, using machine learning to identify and diagnose anomalies in x-rays, and the development of a quantum-inspired PRNG.
